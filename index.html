Supporting Active Military Families

The Gulf Coast is home to Tyndall Air Force Base, Naval Support Activity (NSA PC) and United States Coast Guard Station Panama City. We know frequent moves associated with the military lifestyle can make finding employment a challenge. Our Military Family Employment Program can help.

Along with our partners, CareerSource Gulf Coast provides a variety of employment services and assistance for military families.

Start your job search by registering on EmployFlorida.com.

Our partners and additional resources for military families include:

  • Blue Star Families
  • Military OneSource
  • Florida Department of Veterans’ Affairs
  • Florida Vets First
  • U.S. Department of Labor VETS
  • Department of Veterans Affairs
  • 2019 Florida Military-Friendly Guide
  • Tyndall Air Force Base
  • Panama City Naval Support Activity

Veteran or Veteran’s Family Member?

There’s a jobs portal just for you at
Veterans.EmployFlorida.com. Thank you for your service.


A Beautiful Place to Live and Work

CareerSource Gulf Coast serves Bay, Gulf and Franklin counties. After many community members lost work due to Hurricane Michael, we have worked to rebuild our community. We have established partnerships with local employers looking for reliable employees like you.

We’re glad you’re here and honored to assist you and your family.

CareerSource Gulf Coast is an equal employer/program. Auxiliary aids and services are available upon request to individuals with disabilities. All voice telephone numbers on this website may be reached by persons using TTY/TDD equipment via the Florida Relay Service at 711.
